📄How to Open NPS — Step by Step

Takes 10–15 minutes online
1
Go to eNPS Portal
enps.nsdl.com or via your bank's NPS page (HDFC/ICICI/SBI/Kotak all support)
2
Select Subscriber Type
Individual (All Citizen) or Tier I / Tier II. Use Aadhaar e-KYC for instant activation.
3
Choose Fund Manager & Asset Mix
Select Active Choice → set E/C/G allocation. Or Auto Choice (LC75/LC50/LC25) if you prefer hands-off.
4
Make First Contribution
Min ₹500 for Tier I. Pay via net banking / UPI. PRAN (Permanent Retirement Account Number) generated instantly.

How to buy REITs/InvITs
1. Open a demat account (Zerodha / Groww / HDFC Securities)
2. Search the name on NSE/BSE like a stock
3. Minimum 1 unit (post SEBI rule change)
4. Distributions credited directly to bank account quarterly
5. Consider tranches — use dips in rate cycles for entry
Taxation — REITs & InvITs
● Interest component: Taxed at slab rate (like FD interest)
● Dividend component: Taxed at slab rate
● Return of capital: Not taxed now, reduces cost basis
● Capital gain on sale: STCG 15% (<12 months), LTCG 10% (>12 months)
● Best held in: Lower tax bracket investors get best post-tax yield

🎯Arbitrage Funds — Why & When

The smart liquid-fund replacement for tax efficiency
★Taxed as equity (LTCG 10% after 1Y, STCG 15% before 1Y) — better than debt taxation at slab rate
★Returns track overnight/short-term rates — currently 7–7.5%
★Best in high volatility markets — arbitrage spread widens = higher returns
★Use as parking zone while waiting for equity entry points
★Top funds: Nippon India Arbitrage, HDFC Arbitrage, SBI Arbitrage, Kotak Equity Arbitrage
⚠Avoid when: market very calm (low spread = low returns) or holding period <30 days
Rule of thumb: Use arbitrage funds for any cash you expect to deploy in 3–12 months. After 1 year, switch to equity. It beats FD post-tax for people in the 30% bracket.

📖Step-by-Step Download Guide — Exactly Where to Click

Follow these steps for each platform — all links open in a new tab
Zerodha Equity, ETFs, REITs, InvITs, Bonds, F&O Open Console ↗

1. Go to console.zerodha.com
2. Login with your Zerodha credentials
3. Click Reports in the left sidebar
4. Click Tax P&L
5. Select Financial Year (e.g. FY 2024–25)
6. Click Download → saves as CSV/PDF

1. Go to console.zerodha.com/reports/holdings
2. Login if prompted
3. Your current holdings show on screen
4. Click Download icon (top right) → CSV
5. Or go to Kite app → Portfolio → Holdings
6. Tap the share icon to export
💲 Tradebook
1. Console → Reports → Tradebook
2. Select date range or FY
3. Click Download CSV
4. Contains every buy/sell with price & qty
CDSL — myEASI Demat holdings, REITs, InvITs, ETFs (Zerodha/Groww/Upstox/Angel) Open myEASI ↗
🏛 Demat Holdings Statement
1. Go to easi.cdslindia.com
2. Click Register if first time → use your 16-digit BO ID (from broker)
3. Login with BO ID + Password + OTP
4. Click Statements → Portfolio
5. Select date → Click Download PDF / Excel
6. Shows all stocks, ETFs, REITs, InvITs in demat
📄 CAS (Consolidated Account Statement)
1. Go to cdslindia.com → Investors → CAS
2. Enter your PAN + registered email
3. Select period (monthly / annual)
4. CAS PDF sent to your registered email
5. Covers all demat holdings + MF in one doc
6. Password = first 8 chars of PAN + birthdate (DDMMYYYY)
NSDL — eServices HDFC Securities, ICICI Direct, Kotak, SBI Securities Open NSDL ↗
📋 Holdings / Transaction Statement
1. Go to eservices.nsdl.com
2. Click IDeAS (Investor tab) → Register/Login
3. Use your DP ID + Client ID (from broker)
4. Click Portfolio → View Holdings
5. Click Download → PDF or Excel
6. For transaction history → Statements → Transaction
📄 CAS via NSDL
1. Go to NSDL CAS Download page
2. Enter PAN + email + mobile
3. Choose Detailed or Summary
4. OTP verification → Submit
5. PDF sent to registered email
6. Covers all NSDL demat + MF holdings
MF Statement — CAMS & KFintech All mutual fund folios — Capital Gains, CAS, SIP history Open CAMS ↗
💵 CAS — All MF Folios (CAMS AMCs)
1. Go to camsonline.com → CAS
2. Enter your PAN or registered email
3. Select period: Specific / Since inception
4. Choose Detailed (shows NAV, units, value)
5. Enter email → OTP → Submit
6. PDF arrives in minutes — password = PAN (uppercase)
📈 Capital Gains Statement (for ITR)
1. Go to camsonline.com → Capital Gains
2. Enter PAN + email
3. Select Financial Year (e.g. 2024–25)
4. OTP → Submit → PDF to email
5. Shows STCG & LTCG from all CAMS-serviced AMCs
6. Do the same on KFintech for other AMCs
