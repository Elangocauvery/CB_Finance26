🛢️ GRM — The Single Most Important Metric for Indian Refiners
Same throughput volume: HPCL Q3 FY26 at GRM $5.40/bbl — EBITDA ~₹2,200 Cr. Q4 FY26 at GRM $14.27/bbl — EBITDA ~₹7,500 Cr. A 3.4x swing on identical volume. GRM (Gross Refining Margin) = Product Revenue/bbl minus Crude Cost/bbl minus Variable Opex/bbl. It determines whether a quarter is good or bad for every Indian refiner.
