Q4 FY26 — One-Line Read on Each
Shaily Engineering

Margin, not topline, is the story. Q4 revenue dipped ~5.5% QoQ but PAT +40% as the mix shifted decisively to high-margin healthcare (GLP-1 / pen-injector devices now ~40% of revenue, FY26 healthcare +139%). EBITDA margin 29.3%, ROCE 35.8%, D/E 0.3x. ₹423 Cr pen-injector order + ShailyPen Neo milestone. Premium ~70x+ valuation the only caution. Smallcap World Fund accumulating.

Shakti Pumps

Highest-ever ₹858 Cr quarter (+51% YoY solar-pump installs) but PAT fell 77% — EBITDA margin crashed to 9.7% (from 24.6%) on low Magel Tyala realisation + RM/logistics inflation. FY26 PAT fell 37% despite record revenue. Chairman Patidar framed FY26 as a balance-sheet repair year (receivables cut ₹4,200 Cr). Thesis now hinges on FY27 KUSUM execution + margin recovery.

🔗 Sector & Policy — Structural Tailwinds
These two ride two very different multi-year themes: global GLP-1 / drug-delivery device manufacturing (Shaily) and India's solar-pump / rural-electrification push (Shakti). The policy backdrop below frames each company's runway.
🩺 GLP-1 & Drug-Delivery (Shaily)
Obesity / GLP-1 Supercycle
Global semaglutide / GLP-1 boom (Novo Nordisk, Eli Lilly) drives demand for precision pen injectors — Shaily is a "picks & shovels" device supplier rather than a drug maker.
China+1 / Make-in-India
Global pharma & medtech supply-chain diversification favours Indian precision moulders; semiconductor & consumer-electronics entries add new high-complexity verticals.
Metal-to-Plastic Conversion
Automotive lightweighting & high-performance polymers replacing metal components — structural industrial-segment driver (IATF 16949 certified).
☀ Solar Pumps & Rural Power (Shakti)
PM-KUSUM
Central scheme for solar-powered agricultural pumps; commissioning deadline extended to 31-Mar-2027 — extends Shakti's execution runway. ~25% market share across major states.
PM Surya Ghar / Rooftop Solar
Muft Bijli Yojana drives rooftop-solar adoption; Shakti expanding into rooftop, domestic, industrial & EV-motor segments to diversify beyond agri pumps.
Make-in-India Solar Cells
DCR (domestically-manufactured) cell/module localisation; Shakti's 0.5 GW DCR capacity (Q1 FY27) captures content-localisation policy & reduces import dependence.
