🏆 Best Pick Verdict
Cauvery Business Research investment view — Q4 FY26 updated

Overall #1 Pick: ITC Limited

Even with Q4 FY26 pending, ITC remains the standout value-buy in Indian FMCG. Trading at ~22x P/E vs sector average ~50x, it is the most profitable FMCG company by margin (35% EBITDA, 24.8% net) at the cheapest price. 3.5% dividend yield, massive FMCG scale-up underway, and Hotels demerger optionality. Cigarette tax hike risk is manageable — cigarettes have a history of pricing through every tax cycle.

📢 Cauvery Business Research — Final View (Q4 FY26)

ITC — Our #1 pick. Most undervalued FMCG name in India. Q3 FY26 PAT +9.9%, Q4 pending. Even with cigarette tax headwinds, no other FMCG company offers 35% EBITDA + 3.5% dividend + 22x P/E in India.

Colgate — Our #2 pick for capital efficiency. 83% RoCE, 30%+ EBITDA margins, near-monopoly oral care. The most "boring but brilliant" stock in Indian FMCG.

Surprise of Q4 FY26: Nestlé India — 27% PAT growth, strongest quarter in a decade, 22.6% revenue growth. If you want premium quality with growth, Nestlé justifies its premium valuation this quarter.
