🔄 Old Gold Exchange — Gold4India

  • Launched May 2026 aligned with PM Modi's appeal to reduce gold imports
  • Exchange at 342 India showrooms; target: reduce imports by ~5 tonnes/year
  • Promotes 18K over 22K — affordability at high gold prices

  • ME diaspora customers: old gold exchange widely practised — supports intl. growth

Risk Analysis — Q4 FY26

Sector-wide and company-specific risks — assessed as of May 2026

Sector-Wide Risks

🔴 Gold Import Duty Hike — 6% → 15% (May 2026)
Sudden duty hike raises gold cost for consumers, can dampen discretionary buying, particularly investment-grade purchases (coins, bars). Accelerates shift to old gold exchange — net mixed for retailers.
🔴 High Gold Price — ₹94,000+/10g
Inflated revenues look high but volume growth is modest. Consumer affordability stress. High prices boost exchange-driven sales but compress studded jewellery mix.
🟡 PM Modi Appeal to Reduce Gold Imports
Triggered 22% stock correction in jewellery sector in May 2026. No regulatory change yet, but policy risk remains. Gold4India aligns Kalyan with government intent.
🟡 West Asia Geopolitical Risk
Affects Kalyan's Middle East business (11% revenue). Conflict disrupts customer walk-ins. Titan's Dubai and international stores also impacted intermittently.
🟢 Organised → Unorganised Reversal Risk
BIS hallmarking regulation continues to support organised sector. Risk of reversal is low. Organised share growing from 12% (FY20) to 18% (FY26).

Company-Specific Risks

🔴 Titan — 49x P/E Valuation Risk
EBITDA margin contracted 135bps in Q4 FY26. Any earnings miss at this multiple triggers sharp correction. Watches/Wearables segment soft.
🟡 Kalyan — GML Debt & Franchise Quality
GML debt ₹3,800 Cr. FOCO model risks quality control as network expands to 600+ stores. Promoter holding 62.9% limits re-rating.
🟡 Senco — FY27 Margin Normalisation
FY26 EBITDA 11.5% includes one-off bullion gains. FY27 guidance 7.5–7.8% — 400bps drop will disappoint if investors price FY26 margins as sustainable.
🟡 SGIL — Plant Execution Risk
Mumbai (4,000 kg) and Jaipur (1,200 kg) plants must deliver on time. Any delay collapses the capacity expansion thesis. QoQ production already dipped 13.5% in Q4.
🟡 Goldiam — US Tariff & LGD Price Risk
US tariff escalation could hurt exports (SEEPZ partly mitigates). LGD prices fell 70% in 4 years — if demand slows further, revenue misses. ORIGEM scaling costs a near-term margin drag.
🟢 Thangamayil — TN Concentration
95% revenue from Tamil Nadu. Single-state concentration limits growth ceiling. Gold import duty hike a near-term headwind for TN retail demand.
