⚠️ Key Themes & Risks
✅ Tailwinds
• Complex-generics & biosimilar pipeline monetisation (Shilpa: Orion Nivolumab deal, NorUDCA NCE)
• By-product economics + China+1 in aroma chemicals (Privi 25%+ margins, Givaudan JV profitable)
• Specialty ingredients premiumisation (Sudeep AbsorBis, +62% vertical growth)
• Super-specialty domestic branded growth (Beta oncology own-brands +20%, IVF entry)
• Multi-vertical chemistry platforms scaling (Acutaas: CDMO ramp + fully-contracted electrolyte additives + Indichem semiconductor JV)
• Deleveraging: Privi ND/EBITDA 2.25x → 1.33x; Beta CCDs converted May 2026
⚠️ Headwinds
• Beta: NPPA price cap made Carboplatin (Platins) unviable — ₹30–35 Cr revenue walked away; export tenders slipped to FY27
• Sudeep: margin compression (37.8% → 34.6%) from acid/gas cost spikes + overseas team build-out; working-capital days up
• Shilpa: net debt rose to ₹613 Cr; weak historical ROCE; premium P/E (~50x)
• Privi: promoter stake down 74.05% → 60.60% in a year — stock fell ~8% on results day despite record numbers
• Acutaas: heavy capex + rising working-capital days; ~50x P/E prices in flawless FY27 execution
• Valuations rich across the board after strong runs
🔭 Watch Factors
• Sudeep: Nandesari greenfield customer validation (6–12 months); Dahej iron-phosphate Phase 1 (early CY27); NSS integration
• Beta: export ramp (₹71 Cr → guided ₹105–110 Cr FY27); Nivian consolidation from FY27; EU GMP audit Sep 2026
• Shilpa: Albumin EMA IMPD (H1 FY27); Rotigotine patch EU launch FY27; Abraxane/Enzalutamide FY28
• Privi: 54,000 MTPA capacity by Jun 2026; ethyl maltol/maltol/cyclopentanone project (Jun 2027); 5k:1k vision FY29-30
• Acutaas: electrolyte-additive Phase 2 (Q1 FY27); Indichem Korea plant (H2 CY26); 4 validated CDMO products ramping FY27→FY28

🌸 Privi Speciality Chemicals — Business Model, Verticals & Results
Business Model
Single-segment aroma-chemicals manufacturer converting renewable feedstocks (gum turpentine, crude sulphate turpentine — a pulp-mill by-product) into fragrance ingredients (Amber Fleur, Dihydromyrcenol, Privial, camphor and 50+ molecules) for the world's largest fragrance houses and FMCG majors. The moat is by-product/side-stream economics — every waste stream is monetised, letting Privi earn ~25% EBITDA while supplying customers who know molecule costs to the paisa. Growth stack: dedicated-capacity JV with Givaudan (PRIGIV, 51:49; first such JV in the fragrance industry), capacity expansion to 54,000 MTPA, new molecules (ethyl maltol, maltol, cyclopentanone) and a biomass-conversion demo plant. Merger of Privi Fine Sciences + Privi Biotechnologies in progress (NCLT).
Aroma Chemicals (core) — pinene/terpene chemistry, 100+ countries PRIGIV JV (Givaudan) — ₹55 Cr FY26 → ~₹130 Cr FY27E → ~₹300 Cr in 3–4 yrs New molecules — ethyl maltol · maltol · cyclopentanone (Jun 2027) Biorefinery demo plant — ₹70–75 Cr investment

⚗️ Acutaas Chemicals — Business Model, Verticals & Results
Business Model

Q4 FY26 Earnings Champion: Acutaas Chemicals

The cleanest blowout in this coverage set — Q4 revenue ₹432.8 Cr (+40.3%), gross margin 62% (+1,416 bps), EBITDA margin 42.4% (+1,487 bps) and PAT ₹134.3 Cr (+114%) with no exceptional items, capping an FY26 in which EBITDA doubled and PAT rose 122% to a record ₹356.4 Cr. Growth engines are contracted, not conceptual: battery-additive capacity fully booked for three years, four validated CDMO products queued, and the Indichem Korea semiconductor plant completing H2 CY26. Counterweights: working-capital days and capex are climbing, Q4 seasonality flatters the margin optics (full-year 35.9% is the honest number), and ~50x earnings already assumes the +25% FY27 guide lands. Special mention: Shilpa Medicare (record ₹437 Cr quarter, adjusted FY26 PAT +135% — the platform-inflection story) and Privi Speciality (record year, deleveraging fast, JV turned profitable).

🧑‍⚖️ CB Verdict Cards (Not Recommendations)
Acutaas Chemicals — "Contracted Compounding"
Best-in-set momentum with the rare virtue of clean numbers — no exceptionals, no adjustments needed. The three-vertical build-out (CDMO, battery chemicals, semiconductors) is backed by contracts and validations rather than press releases. The debate: ~50x for a chemicals company, rising WC days, and whether 36% margins survive the capex cycle. Watch: Q1 FY27 (seasonally weakest quarter) as the honest margin test, and Indichem's Korea ramp.
Shilpa Medicare — "Platform Inflection"
The multi-year science bet is finally printing numbers. Best-in-set earnings momentum (CB-A on both Q4 and FY26). The debate is entirely about price (~50x) and whether biologics/NCE optionality justifies it. Watch: Q1 FY27 margin persistence, Albumin EMA filing, Europe formulations run-rate.
Privi Speciality — "Quality Compounder, Promoter Question"
25%+ margins validated by the world's toughest buyers, ROCE 22%, debt falling, 5k:1k roadmap credible via JV + new molecules. But the promoter selling (74% → 60.6%) is a genuine question every investor must answer for themselves; institutional absorption (MFs 1.4% → 9.5%) is one counter-read.
Sudeep Pharma — "Investment-Cycle Year One"
Strong first year as a listed company: +28% revenue, 34%+ margins, specialty mix rising fast. But FY27 is a proving year — greenfield customer validation (6–12 months), NSS integration, and margin recovery must all land, with a battery-materials option maturing only from CY27. Premium valuation (~44x) leaves little room for slippage.
Beta Drugs — "Reset Year, Loaded Spring?"
Headline FY26 looks weak (+4.6%), but the internals show deliberate margin discipline (Platin exit), gross margin +280 bps, exports rebooked for FY27 (+50% guided) and a sensible IVF acquisition consolidating from FY27. The cheapest of the four on earnings — but it must now rebuild guidance credibility after the FY26 miss.
