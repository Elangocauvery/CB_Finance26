Q4 FY26 — One-Line Read on Each
VA Tech Wabag

Clean, consistent compounder. Q4 revenue +22%, PAT +29%, margins steady ~12.5%. ₹17,200 Cr order book = 4.4x revenue. 6th straight year net-cash. New ultra-pure water for semiconductors/data-centres is the next leg. Backed by Rekha Jhunjhunwala (8%).

Bajel Projects

First-ever ₹1,000 Cr+ quarter and PAT +193% — but off a tiny base. EBITDA margin just ~3% and ROE ~3%; a wafer-thin EPC turnaround story. Maiden dividend signals confidence. Auditors flagged ₹86 Cr arbitration. Bajaj Group parentage is the comfort.

CG Power

Record FY26 — revenue +25%, PAT +32% in Q4. Transformer pricing power + ₹17,107 Cr OB (+61%). Debt-free, Murugappa-backed, no pledge. Semiconductor (OSAT Sanand) drags consolidated margins during ramp. Quality is high; valuation (~120x P/E) is the catch.

🔗 Sector & Policy — Structural Tailwinds
These three sit on three of India's strongest multi-year capex themes: water infrastructure, power transmission & distribution, and electrical equipment + semiconductors. The policy backdrop below frames each company's runway.
💧 Water Infrastructure (WABAG)
Jal Jeevan Mission & AMRUT
Multi-lakh-crore central push on drinking water, sewage & reuse. Municipal STP/WTP and desalination demand sustained across India.
Desalination & Reuse
Chennai, GCC and water-stressed regions scaling SWRO desalination; circular-water economy & ZLD norms drive O&M annuity revenue.
Ultra-Pure Water — New Engine
Semiconductor fabs & data centres need ultra-pure water — a new, higher-margin vertical WABAG is positioning for.
⚡ Power T&D (Bajel · CG Power)
National Electricity Plan / Transmission
~₹9 lakh crore transmission capex pipeline to FY32 (PGCIL + state grids + HVDC) to evacuate renewables — direct demand for towers, lines, substations & transformers.
Transformer Super-Cycle
Tight global & domestic transformer capacity; CG Power expanding to 110 GVA by 2026 with strong pricing power. Bajel benefits via substation/T&D EPC.
Data-Centre & Industrial Capex
Data-centre build-out drives GIS substation & switchgear demand (Bajel's Mumbai DC win; CG switchgear greenfield ₹748 Cr).
🔌 Semiconductors & Make-in-India (CG Power)
India Semiconductor Mission
Central + Gujarat incentives for OSAT/ATMP. CG Semi's Sanand facility is among the first operational; 2nd plant under build.
PLI & Localisation
PLI for electrical equipment & components; rising import-substitution in motors, drives, switchgear & railway signalling.
Execution Risk
Semiconductor ramp is capital-heavy and loss-making early — it currently drags CG's consolidated margins vs standalone.
