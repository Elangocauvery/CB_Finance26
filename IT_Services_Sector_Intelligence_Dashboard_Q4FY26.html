The CB IT Rating — results-led scorecard

Five criteria, one star each (max ★★★★★), each scored ★ full · ½ partial · ✗ miss. The three core criteria are hard results parameters with explicit "best" thresholds — revenue growth, operating-margin and net-profit growth. The two hallmark criteria — Innovation/IP and Unique Model — break ties and reward what a company builds and how defensibly it competes. A name earns its premium on results first, then on durability.

1 · Revenue Growth

FY26 INR revenue YoY: ≥12% = full (best) · 7–12% = ½ · <7% = miss.

2 · EBIT / EBITDA Margin

FY26 operating margin: EBIT >20% = full (best) · 17–20% = ½ · <17% = miss. EBITDA ≥22% (or 20–22% = ½) used for product/ER&D names.

3 · Net-Profit Growth

FY26 PAT YoY: ≥12% = full (best) · 5–12% = ½ · <5% or negative = miss.

4 · Innovation & IP HALLMARK

Scaled GenAI revenue, proprietary platforms, patents, R&D depth.

5 · Unique Model & Capital HALLMARK

Defensible, differentiated model + positive FCF, capital returns and deal momentum.
